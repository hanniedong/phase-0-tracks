About the Warriors Dance Team

The Warriors Dance Team has been a dynamic blend of energy, beauty and talent since 1985. Their mission is to entertain their fans and enhance the communities.

Expectations

  • Technically talented dancer, entertainer
  • Ambassador/Representative
  • Positive, energetic, with great personality
  • Physically fit
  • Team player

Commitment

  • Attend and peform at all Warriors home games
  • Able to attend rehearsals on Monday evenings, Wednesday evenings, Sunday afternoons, and additional scheduled rehearsals
  • Available for personal appearances
